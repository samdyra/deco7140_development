Implementation Rationale

Thanks!

First off I just want to mention that I really enjoyed working this assignment 4. It was a really it was a challanging yet fun experience to build a website end to end, from gathering the values, needs, planning the features, learning to design ethical and accessible the UX/UI, and finally implementing the website. Hats off to the teaching team for creating such an engaging and educative course!

Below is my rationale regarding the implementation rationale and justification for the design and features that I have made in this assignment.

From the client brief, the plugin includes several personal data that was not described how its going to be relevant with the goal of the project. Data such as annual income, credit cards, gender,spending habits, monthly dining expenditure will raise serious questions by the users regarding how the data will be used. According to the ACS principle of trustworthiness, professionals are ought to respect privacy and data confidentiality, especially personal data. The current data collected planned in the client brief are cleary appears unnecesarry and invasive.

The client brief noted that the informations gathered from the form will help understand the users for future planning. After careful look, this argument has not been supported with some spesific explanation. This in turn will make it hard for us to justify to the users on why and how the data such as "spending habits" will be used, stored, or shared. The ACS Code mentioned that the importance of how we as a professional must maintain honesty in designing our products or services. Lack of clear disclosure on how we are going to use these personal data will raise high concerns to the users, and therefore might hinder user participation in the project.
Some of the used personal data in the plugin, such as sexuality, gender, and annual income could be a major vulnerability for the community members. Given that your organization will serve multiple diverse communities, and some of them which may have a systemic disadvantage, collecting this kind of data might pose a big threat for them if those data somehow leaked inside or outside of the website that we want to create. As the ACS principle of respect mentioned, respect for others is an essential principle and we have to minimize any harm to stakeholders, including the personal data of the users.
