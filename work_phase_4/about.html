Connected Communities Initiative

Connected Communities Initiative is a non-profit organisation dedicated to fostering social cohesion by empowering diverse communities to share their stories, connect with others, and access resources that enhance their visibility and influence.

Our mission is to strengthen societal bonds by creating platforms that celebrate and support the unique identities of communities across Australia. We commission the development of dynamic, user-friendly websites that serve as central hubs where community members can share resources, highlight events, and foster meaningful connections.

Each platform we support is designed with accessibility, inclusivity, and ethical design at its core, ensuring that every community has the tools to express their authentic identity and build lasting connections.

Our Core Values

FeedCrumbs was developed with four core values that guide every design and functionality decision:

01

Personal & Community Identity Representation

Empowering users to express their authentic cultural identity through traditional cuisines and shared stories.

02

Easy to Use and Accessible Resources

Providing intuitive navigation and helpful resources to ensure everyone can participate and learn.

03

Create Meaningful Connections

Building bridges between diverse communities through shared appreciation of food and cultural traditions.

04

Inclusivity, Accessibility, and Cultural Sensitivity

Designing with WCAG standards and ethical considerations to respect all backgrounds and abilities.
