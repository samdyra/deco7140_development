Style Guide

This is the Style Guide for the FeedCrumbs website, focusing on the three levels of design: Visceral, Behavioral, and Reflective.

Visceral Design

The immediate emotional response to visual design. Focuses on aesthetics, first impressions, and sensory appeal.

  • Layout and Wireframe
  • Color palette
  • Typography and spacing

Behavioral Design

The experience of use, functionality, usability, and interaction patterns. Includes how users navigate and interact with the design.

  • Navigation and user flows
  • High fidelity components
  • Responsive design patterns

Reflective Design

The meaning and personal feeling of the design. Includes how users perceive the brand and their emotional connection to it.

  • Content strategy and messaging
  • Brand identity and values
  • User engagement and retention
