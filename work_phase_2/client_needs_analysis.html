The Client Brief

About Us

We are a non-profit organisation dedicated to fostering social cohesion by empowering diverse communities to share their stories, connect with others , and access resources that enhance their visibility and influence. Our mission is to strengthen societal bonds by creating platforms that celebrate and support the unique identities of communities across Australia.

This brief has been prepared for the internal design teams at Connect Communities Initiative (CCI). Each design team is tasked with developing individual concepts and prototypes for dynamic websites that align with CCI's mission and vision. Our mission is to strengthen societal bonds by creating platforms that celebrate and support the unique identities of communities across Australia.

Project Overview

We are commissioning the development of dynamic, user-friendly websites that represent and support different communities. Each website should serve as a central hub where community members can share resources, highlight events, and foster meaningful connections. The websites must not include any form of commercial activity, such as advertising or selling products. paHowever, it is acceptable to promote resources, events, or services that are directly beneficial to the community. s

The websites must be accessible, mobile-friendly, and reflect the diverse needs of the selected community focus area while adhering to our mission of inclusivity, accessibility, and ethical design. Each website should also celebrate the unique characteristics of its chosen community and support meaningful interaction and engagement.

Community Focus Areas

  • Cultural and Heritage Communities
  • Academic and Professional Communities
  • Wellness and Mental Health Communities
  • Social and Environmental Advocacy Communities
  • Regional or International Communities
  • Creative Arts and Media Communities
  • Lived Experience Communities
  • Food and Culinary Communities
  • Tech and Innovation Communities

Target Audience

Each website must serve two key groups:

  • Community Members: Individuals within the selected community who seek representation, resources, and connection.
  • General Audience: Broader society, who can use the website to learn about the community and its contributions.

Accessibility must be prioritised to ensure seamless interaction for all users, including those relying on assistive technologies.

Project Goals

  • Represent and celebrate the identity of the chosen community, recognising and representing the diversity within the community rather than relying on stereotypes.
  • Provide clear, accessible information and resources to users.
  • Foster meaningful connections and support within the community.
  • Ensure ethical, inclusive, and culturally sensitive design principles are upheld.

Values - Needs - Feature Analysis

Value 1: Personal & Community Identity Representation Through Cultural Cuisines

Card 1

Users need to express their personal identity through their cultural cuisines

Features:

  • User profile pages with cultural background sections
  • Users can create personal resources (posts, recipes) to express their ideas

Card 2

Users need to express their own community culture through traditional cuisines

Features:

  • Feature to Create, join, and share communities
  • Community groups can express their culture through multiple ways like recipes, events, stories

Value 2: Easy to use and Accessible Resources

Card 1

Users need easy access to access resources.

Features:

  • Promote relevant posts, recipes, and events
  • Interactive map showing geo located posts, events, and authentic recipes for each region in Australia
  • A Wiki section for all diverse community groups within the community

Card 2

Users need beginner friendly guides and learning materials

Features:

  • Getting started guides with step-by-step instructions in main pages
  • Gamifications of some things in order to make the website fun to use

Value 3: Create Meaningful Connections

Card 1

Users need to connect with others with various interests and cultural backgrounds

Features:

  • Discussion forums with various communities
  • Timeline based system to share traditional cuisines, ideas, and events

Card 2

Users need to create and participate in community events

Features:

  • Event creation platform for community organized events
  • Photos or other form of documentation sharing about the events

Card 3

Users need to share contents and receive feedback from the community

Features:

  • Social feed with posts, comments, and reactions (similiar to most of social media)
  • Recipe rating and review system

Value 4: Inclusivity, Accessibility, and Cultural Sensitivity

Card 1

Users with visual disabilities need accessible website

Features:

  • WCAG 2.2 AA compliance
  • Alt text for images, captions for videos, adjustable text size

Card 2

Users with limited English or tech savvyness need simple and informative interfaces

Features:

  • Plain language content (Grade 8 reading level)
  • step by step guides
  • Mobile-first responsive design with large touch targets

Card 3

The client needs culturally sensitive contents to ensure respectful and accurate representation

Features:

  • Content moderation guidelines promoting authentic representation
  • Collaboration with community leaders for content review
  • Feedback mechanism for users to report concerns
