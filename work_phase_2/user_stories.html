User Stories

Interview 1: Blake

Initial Prompt:

Can you tell me about a time when you felt a strong sense of community? What made that experience memorable for you?

Follow-up Question:

How do you think technology can enhance or hinder the sense of community in today's world?

Response:

I remember a local festival in my neighborhood where everyone came together to celebrate our diverse cultures. It was memorable because it wasn't just about the event itself, but the way people interacted, shared stories, and supported each other. Technology can enhance community by connecting people who might not otherwise meet, but it can also hinder it if it leads to superficial interactions or replaces face-to-face connections.

Follow-up Question:

What features would you like to see in a community-focused website that would make it more engaging and useful for you?

Response:

I would love to see features like event calendars, forums for discussions, and resource sharing sections. They would make the website more engaging and useful by providing practical tools for interaction and information exchange.
