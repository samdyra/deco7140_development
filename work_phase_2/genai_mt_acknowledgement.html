2. Context and Purpose

Briefly explain the purpose of the task and what you were trying to achieve with the help of AI Tools — or what guided your decision not to use them. “I used Claude to generate an outline for my report so I could compare it to my own initial structure and improve flow between sections.”

3. Critical Decision-Making and Ownership

Explain how you stayed in control of the process and made informed decisions about what to keep, change, or reject. Show how your final submission reflects your authorship. “I rewrote the AI-generated paragraph to improve technical accuracy and match the tone of the client. The suggestion was useful as a starting point, but not appropriate as written.” “I commented each section of code generated by Copilot to verify my understanding before adapting the logic to my own HTML structure.”

4. Accuracy, Bias, and Ethical Awareness

Demonstrate how you verified information and ensured the content was accurate, inclusive, and appropriate for your audience. “The AI suggested a leaderboard feature, but I rejected this because it conflicted with user privacy concerns identified in my interviews.” “I double-checked definitions provided by [AI Tool] against official documentation and amended those that were overly simplified or misleading.”

5. Privacy and Sensitive Content

Show that you avoided entering confidential or identifiable information in your prompts. “Before using ChatGPT to generate summary bullet points, I stripped all names and personal details from user quotes.”

6. Reflection on Learning

Reflect on how AI Tool use (or your decision not to use them) supported your learning and understanding of the task. “Exploring AI-generated examples helped me think more clearly about accessibility trade-offs, even though I didn’t use the final outputs directly.”

7. Attribution and Final Statement

Close your acknowledgment with a clear integrity statement that affirms your responsible use and ownership of the final submission. “I used [AI Tool] to support my learning by generating alternative ideas and refining structure. All outputs were critically reviewed, adapted where appropriate, and integrated thoughtfully. I maintained control and ensured that all final work reflected my understanding and capabilities.”
